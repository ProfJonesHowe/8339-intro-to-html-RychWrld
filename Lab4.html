Greek myths

Something I am interested in is Greek mythology. I find it fascinating because it shares certain aspects with other religions and mythologies. What stands out to me is how the gods are portrayed as being very similar to humans, with the main difference being their powers. The myths often highlight the flaws and struggles of these gods, making them relatable in a unique way. One example is how some myths even begin with conflicts between parents and their children.
